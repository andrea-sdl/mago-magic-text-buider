FAQ

How do I start with empty textareas?

just add the clear=true to the url or bookmark http://mago.haptime.in?clear=true

How do I activate csv by default?

just add the csv=true to the url or bookmark http://mago.haptime.in?csv=true

Can I mix them both?

Oh yeah! http://mago.haptime.in?clear=true&csv=true

Why did you build something like this?

I needed it. Sometimes I wanted to build some complex queries based on data, but I hate using excel to do that since it doesn't help you visualize how you can print the data.

Does MAGO works offline?

Yes! If your browser supports html5 offline capabilities, then you're good to go.
